2. playfair
================================================================================

Role in package : Source files redistributed (and used) by iMirror for AirPlay
                  FairPlay SAP handling. Shipped at:
                      ThirdParty/playfair/*.c, ThirdParty/playfair/*.h

License         : GNU General Public License, version 3 (GPLv3).
                  License text: ThirdParty/playfair/LICENSE.md

Corresponding source (GPLv3 §6):
                  The playfair source is itself included in the package under
                  ThirdParty/playfair/, which constitutes the corresponding
                  source for this component.

Note            : playfair is GPLv3 and is COMBINED with iMirror — its sources
                  are shipped in the package and read at runtime by
                  MacMirrorReceiver.Networking/AirPlayPlayFair.cs to obtain the
                  FairPlay tables, which are required for real-device mirroring.
                  iMirror is therefore non-functional without playfair, making
                  the distributed work a GPLv3 combined work. This is the
                  determining reason iMirror as a whole is licensed under GPLv3
                  (see the repository LICENSE and
                  docs/specs/open-source-strategy.md). The combined-work licensing
                  question is thus RESOLVED: the whole is GPLv3.
